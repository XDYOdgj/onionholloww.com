Elegant Iron Burlap Lamp Shades

Enhance your interior with these stylish chandelier lamp shades crafted from iron burlap textured fabric. Their unique, elegant look brings a touch of sophistication and modern artistry to your decor. The empire shape offers a balanced and striking visual, perfect for adding character to any room.

Featuring a durable brass clip-on fitting, these shades are designed for seamless installation on chandeliers and are compatible with bare torpedo bulbs. With a 3" top x 4" bottom x 4" slant height, they ensure an ideal fit and proportional design that complements your lighting setup.

Made with virgin styrene backing board, these shades provide long-lasting durability while enhancing natural light diffusion. They offer a warm and inviting glow without the risk of cracking or yellowing over time, making them a reliable choice for both residential and commercial use.

Whether you're upgrading your chandelier or enhancing a specific lighting area, these lamps shades are versatile enough to work with accent lighting, sconces, or candlestick designs. Their functionality and aesthetic combination elevate any interior space with a refined, timeless feel.
