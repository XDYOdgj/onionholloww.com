Heavy Duty 12-Inch Gate Stopper Hardware

This durable gate stopper hardware is constructed from thick gauge iron steel and features a long-lasting grey powder coating. It's designed to withstand harsh weather conditions and resist corrosion, making it an excellent choice for outdoor use. Whether you're securing a gate vertically or horizontally, this hardware ensures stability and keeps your property protected with confidence.

The unique upper ear design on the rod prevents the pole from slipping out, providing added safety and reliability. It's compatible with a variety of gate materials, including wood, metal, and vinyl, offering a versatile and strong solution for gate stabilization. Ideal for both single and double gate setups, this stopper hardware allows you to hold one gate in place while latching the other, ensuring smooth and secure operation.
