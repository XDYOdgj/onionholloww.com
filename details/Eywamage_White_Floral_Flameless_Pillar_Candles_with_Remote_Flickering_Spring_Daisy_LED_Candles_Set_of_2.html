Eywamage White Floral Flameless Pillar Candles

Eywamage White Floral Flameless Pillar Candles are the perfect mix of elegance and safety, combining the beauty of real candles with the convenience of modern technology. These realistic, battery-powered candles feature a lifelike 3D black wick and a smooth paraffin wax coating, giving them a natural, authentic appearance without the risk of fire or smoke.

Enhance your living space with these charming, spring-inspired candles as they add a delicate floral touch to any room. The soft white daisy flowers create a timeless, romantic ambiance, making these candles ideal for decoration or as a thoughtful gift for loved ones.

With a remote control feature, you can easily adjust the lighting settings, choose between flickering and static flame modes, customize brightness levels, and set timers for 2, 4, 6, or 8 hours. This added flexibility makes the candles perfect for both everyday use and special events.

Each candle is designed to last for over 350 hours when using 2 AA batteries (sold separately), ensuring long-lasting illumination. These safe and windproof candles are perfect for indoor use, whether you're decorating your home, hosting a party, or simply enjoying a relaxing evening in.

The two different sizes offer varied placement options and create a striking visual contrast, making them versatile for any setting. Whether in a living room, patio, or event space, these candles bring warmth, charm, and a touch of nature to your environment without the hassle of open flames or maintenance.
