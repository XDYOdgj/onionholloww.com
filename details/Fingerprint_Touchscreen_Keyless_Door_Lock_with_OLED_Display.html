Modern Smart Door Lock with Fingerprint and Touchscreen Features

Experience the future of home security with this advanced smart door lock. Designed for seamless integration into your daily life, it combines fingerprint recognition, a touchscreen interface, and keyless entry in one elegant solution.

Smart and Secure Design

This fingerprint touchscreen keyless door lock includes an intuitive OLED display that simplifies the process of adding or removing users, ensuring a hassle-free setup. It also features a double verification mode that requires a user code and fingerprint or two different fingerprints to unlock, adding an extra layer of security to protect your home.

Versatile Unlocking Options

With its 5-in-1 functionality, this smart lock offers multiple ways to open your door, including fingerprint, code, smartphone, knock/shake, and mechanical key. Whether you're in a rush or need an alternative method, this lock adapts to your lifestyle while keeping your door secure.

Built for Every Door

Perfect for doors with thickness between 40mm and 120mm, this lock is compatible with both right and left-handed handles thanks to its reversible design. The idle handle system prevents forced entry, while the turn handle ensures effortless deadbolt locking. Its built-in smart alarm and voice guide keep your experience smooth and user-friendly.

Stay Connected and Informed

Whether you're at home or away, this keyless entry lock provides convenience and peace of mind. The smart features keep you updated on battery levels and guide you through setup with ease, making it a reliable choice for modern smart homes.
